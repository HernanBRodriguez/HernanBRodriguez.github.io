Teaching Experience & Courses

I have over twenty-five years of university-level teaching experience, progressing through different roles including Undergraduate Teaching Assistant (1998–2002), Graduate Teaching Assistant (2002–2010), and Lecturer / Head of Practical and Laboratory Instruction (2010–2023). Since 2023, I have held a permanent position as Assistant Professor (Profesor Adjunto) in the Department of Inorganic, Analytical, and Physical Chemistry, Faculty of Exact and Natural Sciences, University of Buenos Aires. Throughout my career, I have been responsible for coordinating laboratory courses, supervising teaching assistants, and delivering both theoretical and practical instruction at undergraduate and postgraduate/graduate levels.

As a professor, I have delivered courses in the following areas at both undergraduate and postgraduate/graduate levels:

.

  • General Chemistry (undergraduate)
  • Physical Chemistry (undergraduate)
  • Photochemistry and Spectroscopy (postgraduate) / Molecular Photochemistry (undergraduate)
